# Security Policy

Thank you for helping keep jacobsmxth.dev and its users safe!

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you believe you've found a security vulnerability, please send a report to:
security@jacobsmxth.dev

I will respond within 48 hours and work with you to understand and address the issue.

## Scope

All services and applications hosted on:
- jacobsmxth.dev
- *.jacobsmxth.dev

## Out of Scope

- Third-party services (GitHub, Vercel, etc.)
- Social engineering
- Physical security

## Safe Harbor

I support security research conducted in good faith and will not pursue legal action
for security research that follows responsible disclosure practices.
